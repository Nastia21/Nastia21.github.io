Features of the order
The cost of delivery, decoration and additional ingredients is calculated individually by the manager after filling out the application.

The cake must be ordered at least 3 days before the event. It is better to order in advance so that we have time to prepare it for sure.

If you have difficulty choosing the taste of the filling when ordering, we provide free tasting kits that you can take home and taste slowly in a comfortable environment for you.

To order, we accept cakes from 2 kilograms. Two-tiered cakes - from 4 kilograms, Three-tiered cakes - from 7 kilograms.

We decorate cakes: with mastic, mirror glaze, cream patterns, photo prints, macarons and cookies, fresh flowers and berries (according to the season), canned berries, additional inedible decor (on demand).

The design of the cake can be made from a photograph, combined from several photographs, or according to your sketch or description. If necessary, we help with the selection of the cake design.

Here you can order a cake of any size from 1.5 to 150 kg - it's not a problem!
