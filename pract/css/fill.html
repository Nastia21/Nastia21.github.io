Question & Answers


How and for how long can you store the cake?
Any of our cake can be perfectly stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. But there are a few tricks. If you plan to pick up the cake in the evening before the holiday, then wrap the box with the cake in cling film - this will allow it not to absorb excess odors and not dry out. And before eating, give it some time at room temperature. Keep the cake away from sunlight and heaters.
Can I change / cancel my order?
Yes, but not later than 4 days before the execution date. Contact us in a convenient way and be sure to wait for confirmation.
Do you need to place an order for a while?
As soon as possible. The fact is that in the coming dates there are almost always no places, so it is desirable to anticipate everything in advance. Busy and open dates can be seen in the calendar in the "Orders" section.
Is it possible to make a cake with mastic?
No, and this is a principled position. There are many reasons: it is not modern, harmful (natural mastic is practically not used), greatly reduces the useful weight of the cake (mastic is usually thrown away, which is about 1/4 of the weight + figures). Instead, we can cover the cake with cream or chocolate ganache and put edible gingerbread - see photos of our work.

How to transport a cake?
It is best to transport the cake on a flat car floor. In the heat, be sure to turn on the air conditioner. The higher the cake, the more carefully you have to go so that the cakes do not shift, so natural cakes are much harder than store-bought.
