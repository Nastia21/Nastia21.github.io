WEDDING CAKES

A wedding cake is not just a tradition, but a very important part of the wedding! You can order a beautiful and delicious wedding cake from us! We will approach the choice of cake design with great responsibility and offer you the most delicious fillings.

No matter the size, your wedding celebration deserves a beautiful and delicious cake.
A wedding cake prepared to order in our pastry shop will become a real work of culinary art.

We offer:

  1. Mastic or creamy dessert design;
  2. Decorations in the form of figurines or sugar flowers;
  3. Covering with glaze;
  4. Decoration with flower arrangements, berries, fruits, cream patterns;
  5. Decoration with colorful and even humorous compositions from mastic;
  6. Making multi-tiered cakes;
  7. Application of any photo to the surface of the product.

Before ordering a wedding cake, you have to choose the filling for it. By the way, we provide 12 options for the internal filling of the treat. If you have any difficulties with the choice when making an application, then our employees will be happy to advise you and answer all your questions online or by phone.
